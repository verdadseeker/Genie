# The Movement — Document Index

**Manish's mission: become a well-known figure who helps people remember they're already divine — a blend of Krishna, Buddha, Osho, Nietzsche, Kanye, and Tony Robbins, delivered through embodied practice and hip-hop culture.**

- **The belief:** You are not broken. You are a god who forgot.
- **The method:** State first, story second — change the body (breath, sound, strength, sexuality, devotion) and the story rewrites itself.
- **The medium:** Culture, not a classroom. Celebration, hip-hop, art, spectacle.
- **The line:** No guru, no saint, no ashram, never boring.
